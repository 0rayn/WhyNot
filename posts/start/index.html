-> Introduction:

This is the first blog-post of a serie that’s going to document my journey developing my own operating system, each blog-post under the OSDEV tag will tackle a concept/component of the OS, while having sub-tags to make it easier to search for the multi-parts blogs of the same concept.

-> What to expect and not to expect from this blog:

  • Expect:
    • showcasing milestone of the Yona Operating system.
    • Resources and tips about the OS components.
    • Explaining what I can along the way.
    • A weird way of doing things (I guess).
  • Just don’t think about it:
    • Thinking of this as an all in 1 guide or a step by step tutorial to making your OS, you’ll need to search and learn more.
    • A copy paste paradise :).
    • This is not a “HOW TO DO X”, it’s how I understood it and how I did it.
    • a bug free solution :) (If you found one report it please).
